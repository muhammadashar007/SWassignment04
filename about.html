About Us

We at AT STORE specialise in the importing and exporting of new and second hand car parts all over the world.

Situated in the heart of the midlands, as a newly formed company we are expanding rapidly.

We handle hundreds of car parts daily and sell parts directly to the consumer, motor dealers and traders from all over the world.

We have links to breakers from across the globe and can import you car parts should you require.

Customer can purchase via mail order, over the phone or internet or call into our site directly.

All our parts are kept on site, however if a part requires importation, customers will be informed prior to purchase.

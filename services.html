Services

Services provided
  • On site and in warehouse stored on the shelf second hand car parts
  • Importation of used or new car parts when required at customers request (service usually takes 4 weeks to order part from Japan, Germany, France or America)
  • Part locator (new or used) service available if you cannot find your part.
  • Tyre locater service can be provided in new or part worn condition
  • Recovery service available upon request
  • Advice given free of charge at all times
  • Walk in service available with parts for ready for collection with prior notice
